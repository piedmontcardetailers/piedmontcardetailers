About Us

We are a car detailing and hand wash service located in Piedmont, California. After carefully observing the state of many luxury cars in the Piedmont area, we identified a prevalent issue: the lack of adequate cleaning and protection. Our mission is to provide top-notch services to those vehicles. We specialize in thorough exterior cleaning and hand waxing, ensuring that your vehicle receives the care and attention it deserves. Options

Simple Clean - $20

A simple rinse and non-contact wash that removes the majority of the dirt buildup on the car. Followed with a dry that prevents watermarks and a finishing ceramic detailer coat.

Deep Clean - $40

A deep hand wash that removes all of the dirt buildup on the top paint layer. A scrub down with a clay-bar that removes all contaminants in the paint and significantly reduces the size of scratches or removes them altogether. A window wash inside and outside followed by a rim clean. After a headlight clean, (if needed) Finish with a layer of ceramic detailer coat.

Deep Clean and Hand Wax - $60

A deep clean as described above with a deep hand wax that protects the paint from watermarks and gives it a rich color that pops.

Interior Clean - $20

A light interior clean that removes dirt and grime from the carpets, removes dust from the front of the car and the console, and leaves the seats well cleaned.
